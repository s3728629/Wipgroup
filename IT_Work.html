IT Work - An Interview Transcript

Interview Questions – Conducted at 2:30pm-3pm on Monday 27/04/2020

Shreenu Bhaskar KPMG – Senior Consultant


1. Please tell us about your IT work. What exactly do you do?

Technology Advisor at KPMG. Gives advice about different technologies and systems that can be used for processes, support business objectives and goals. Better serve customer and clients.

Generally, will work with Chief Information Officer, Chief Technology Officer, business analysts, managers, process, data related roles and other various stakeholders.

High level, strategy for clients.

Might talk to client facing staff to see the journey customers go through and find pain points.


2. Please tell us about the industry you work in.

No specific industry.

Different clients in different industries, various Victorian government departments, CFA (Country Fire Authority), financial services, big banks, superannuation funds, universities, hospitals and the health industry, power and utility companies.


3. What other kinds of work do you have to do?

Technology strategies, process improvements, collaborating with people in different areas, applications, systems. Road maps of activity, transition for large scale projects.

Projects tech strategy work, different size projects.


4. Who are all the different people you interact with in your work? Please tell us about them.

Virtually everyone possible. Clients, stakeholders, different departments if needed SME(subject matter experts) project management, managers and executives. Sometimes frontline staff to see the customer journey. Generally higher-level project teams and senior staff at clients.


5. Please tell us about your interactions with other IT professionals.

If they don’t have the specialists needed in the business, they have relationships with third party organization’s they can use as IT SMEs (subject matter experts). The do use different areas as SMEs but will generally only be involved in that aspect of the project either speaking with the project team or the client on their particular area. Eg Cybersecurity hot topic at the moment.


6. What about your interactions with clients or investors?

Face-face generally, fact finding, go to their offices to be a presence. Also, to be there day to day to get a feel of how things are. Also, to conduct stakeholder interviews at the various levels or areas needed.


7. What aspects of your work do you spend most time on? Please tell us about these.

Building stakeholder relationships, making sure client is happy the way they are delivering the work. It needs to be tailored and relevant to issues, problems, and the deliverables they want.


8. Which aspects of your work do you find most challenging?

Nuances each business or organization has. This has been different goals and focuses, can be challenging when stakeholders disagree. Senior stakeholders disagreeing, sometimes they may be engaged to look at improvement in an area that can then be presented as an independent assessment that this area needs to be improved.


9. Finally, can you share an example of the work you do that best captures the essence of the IT industry?

Given Covid-19, there have been contact with different government departments to expand system abilities and support. This best captures the essence of the IT industry as it has limitation and as expectations change, so does the IT infrastructure.
